Mission High Foundation Spring '15
Inauguration Dinner

6:00pm, Wednesday May 13th @ Mission High School, SF CA

Buy a ticket now

Dinner Details

An inspiring entertaining evening that promises to provide a unique and intimate opportunity to experience Mission High School student passion and creativity in the company of Mission High students, faculty, parents, friends and partners.

Rachel Vigil, Mission High School Food and Agricultural Coordinator and Urban Agriculture Teacher, and her students will prepare and serve a wonderful 3-course meal prepared with vegetables grown in the school’s on-campus organic farm. The evening will also feature work from students in other MHS programs and departments.
