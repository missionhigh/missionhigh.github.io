Mission High Foundation
Spring '16 Dinner

6:00pm, Wednesday May 11th @ Mission High School, SF CA

Dinner Details

An inspiring and entertaining evening that offers an intimate experience of Mission High School, including the passion and creativity of its students, faculty, parents, friends, and partners.

Rachel Vigil, Mission’s outstanding Food and Agricultural Coordinator and Urban Agriculture Pathway Teacher, alongside local restaurants chefs will again prepare an extraordinary 3-course meal, including farm-to-table vegetables from the school’s organic farm, grown specifically for the dinner.
